Going Long or Short

You can potentially profit from falling market prices by making a Sell bet – known as going short. For example, should you think that the GBP/USD is going to fall, you can place a Sell trade to potentially profit if the market does fall in price.

Conversely, if you think the market is going to rise you make a Buy bet – known as going long.
