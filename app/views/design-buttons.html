Buttons

Buttons are the main way users interact with the pages. There may be other elements, but these should be the most obvious and easiest understood.

Primary buttons are the main action in any context, and there should only be one. It must be labelled, and may have an additional indicator.

Secondary buttons are for us mostly the Sell and Buy buttons. In most places where we show live prices for markets, the area is a button which will initiate a trade in that direction.

Note: use commas in 1000s for prices if not in currency?

There are other buttons that function as controls, some of these are toggle switches which highlight to show their activity and others are simple actions.
