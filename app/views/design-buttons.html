Text

Designing a new platform is an opportunity to unify our font choices around a modern set of typographic rules.

For GAIN CAPITAL trading platforms we should look at standardising on a more modern screen font. City Index has already been using Open Sans for their marketing collateral, and this is a good choice for platforms too.
