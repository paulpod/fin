Placing a Trade using the Dealing Ticket

  1. 1. Select [Buy] or [Sell] for the trade direction you want to take.
  2. 2. Enter the amount to trade in the Quantity field.
  3. 3. Add stop loss and take profit orders as needed and check the margin required to place the trade. Click [Place Trade] when ready.






Platform Features

  • Watchlists
  • Monitoring Trades with the Open Positions Panel
  • Checking final P&L with the Trade History Panel
