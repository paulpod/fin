Price Alerts

Alerts can be set on a market to notify you when the price hits a certain level. The alerts can be sent within the platform or as a push notification.


Generic image of Alert tab on Trade/Order ticket

  1. Access the Alert tab from the Trade/Order ticket of the market where you want to set an alert.
  2. Set whether you want the alert to be triggered by the buy or sell price reaching a certain level.
  3. Enter the price level that triggers the alert and how long you want the alert to be active.
