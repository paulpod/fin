Search = browse + find

Finding a market to trade in is a core feature for alpha. We are enhancing the search field to cater to two user needs:

FINDING User is looking for a specific market to research and trade in, we should get them there as fast as possible

BROWSING User does not really have something in mind as they activate the search field, will begin thinking as they begin typing
