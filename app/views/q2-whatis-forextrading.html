What is Forex Trading?

Foreign exchange – commonly known as Forex or FX, is the exchange of one currency for another at an agreed exchange price. Traders speculate on whether the value of a currency such as pound sterling, will go up or down in relation to another currency such as the US dollar.


An Example

Let’s say our current GBP/USD market is priced at:

1.4193

The Result

You bought at 1.4193 and sold at 1.4223, which represents a positive 30 point movement in your favour. At your stake size of 100,000 this results in a profit of £300.
