Short "How to...?" guide on using advanced platform features.

Platform Workspaces

Customise the platform to suit your preferences. You can save several arrangements into a platform workspace, allowing you to quickly switch between different arrangements as required.



  1. Modify the platform arrangement as desired.
  2. Select Save current layout and enter the name you want to call the workspace.
  3. All saved workspaces now appear in the drop down menu enabling you to switch between them quickly.
