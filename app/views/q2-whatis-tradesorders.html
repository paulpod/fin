Trades vs Orders

A “trade” is when you buy or sell a market immediately at the current buy or sell price.

An “order” is an instruction to make a trade when specific conditions are met and nothing otherwise. Orders can be an “opening order” or a “closing order”.

Opening orders are used to automatically place a trade when the market reaches a user specified price.

Closing orders are attached to trades and close the trade when a condition is met, for example: when the market reaches a user specified price.

Margin Requirements

There is a minimum margin required for each market in order to open and maintain a trade. The margin requirement to open a trade in shown in the Margin Calculator section of every trade ticket, so you know how much is needed before you place the trade.
