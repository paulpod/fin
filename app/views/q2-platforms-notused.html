Trading Basics

What is Trading?

Trading or speculating in the World’s financial and commodity markets is a bet on whether the price of a market will go up or down in the near future.

A market can be almost anything from gold, currencies, shares in a company, or oil. Your profit or loss in the trade depends on how much the market moves, and in which direction – up or down.
