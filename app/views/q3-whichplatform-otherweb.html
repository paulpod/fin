Watchlists

A watchlist is where you can monitor your favourite markets and you can create many different ones as required. A default Popular Markets list is provided for you.



  1. 1. Starting from the Popular Markets list, you can create any additional lists as needed.
  2. 2. Click a star next to any market to add it to another watchlist.
  3. 3. Click the Sell or Buy price to trade any market.




Platform Features

  • Watchlists
  • Dealing Ticket – Placing a Trade
  • Open Positions Panel – Monitoring Trades
  • Trade History Panel – Checking Final P&L
  • Market Search
  • Charts
